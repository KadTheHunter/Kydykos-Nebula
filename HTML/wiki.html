Wiki

This page (and it's subsequent pages) document the history and lore of the Kædykos Nebula and it's related subjects. Previous iterations were kept as a Fandom Wikia, historically maintained by Kad the Hunter, followed by his successor Cade, but due to Fandoms bloated design and heavy advertising, combined with the lack of a need for community contribution, Cades successor Sir Kaddicus III moved the project to this site instead.
