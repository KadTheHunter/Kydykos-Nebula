Welcome to the Kædykos Nebula.

Established by the ancient Vesper, it is a place for anyone who has a sense of adventure, and a healthy admiration of common sense.

Currently headed by Sir Kaddicus III, a direct descendant of the ancient Vesper himself, Kædykos Nebula has seen near unlimited growth and prosperity under his rule, with outposts, fleets, and archives being built, alliances established with groups such as Parsa Corp Inc., and initiatives like Lazy Builders Inc. being started and funded.
