Parsa Corp. Inc.

Parsa Corp Inc. is a company based out of the Indu-Corp Sector, producing a variety of technological and scientific products, notably their Portable Storage Devices.

Origins

PCI started in 1997 when a small hut popped into existence. Not long after, it was discovered by Parsa__, who claimed it as the HQ for PCI. In the first few years, PCI had next to nothing; Just a desk, some blueprints, and a handful of spare parts.

Over the course of several years, Parsa__ worked alone in that hut, designing new technology and devices, until the hut suddenly vanished, leaving behind nothing but the desk and blueprints.

Taking this as a sign of sorts, Parsa__ ventured out to pitch some of the ideas. With the success of the pitches, particularly the Portable Storage Device, PCI truly began to grow.

Expansion

Following the success of the Portable Storage Device, PCI expanded its operations into a small office, where Parsa__ began to design more devices.

During this time, an experiment with a space bender went horribly wrong, resulting in the destruction of PCI Headquarters, as well as nearly all of the blueprints stored there.

Following this disaster, Parsa__ ventured to the Kædykos Nebula and met Sir Kaddicus III, who introduced her to Lazy Builders Inc.

Present Day

In the Kædykos Nebula, PCI contracted Lazy Builders Inc. to build a series of Quartz Towers that would serve as PCIs new Headquarters.

Today, PCI and it's owner Parsa__ are based out of the Indu-Corp Sector and primarily serve customers in the Kædykos Nebula, Hakani Space, and the Zyrkrok Hive Nest.
