Sir Kaddicus III

Basically, it's me. I'm Sir Kaddicus III. I wrote this entire Wiki, cataloging events of the past, present and even in some ways, future. History does repeat itself after all.

A lot of things have changed, both in and out of Lore, and I don't feel like adding on to or even maintaining this anymore.
Is this yet another project I have started and subsequently lost interest in, forming a slightly concerning pattern of chronic disinterest and neglect? Yes.
Do I care? No.

In-Lore reason I left/quit:
I disappeared into the multiverse whilst hunting down Abyss Neurax, and haven't been seen since.

Out-Lore reason I left/quit:
After starting a new job, I have less time for stuff like this, and in retrospect some most ok all of it was cringe to a degree, and shouldn't have been made at all, ever.
