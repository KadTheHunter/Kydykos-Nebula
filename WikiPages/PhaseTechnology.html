Phase Technology

Phase Technology is a highly advanced tunneling and teleportation system, originally designed to transport bulk goods instantaneously. Subsequent Generations of Phase Technology have expanded to allow transportation of individual items, complex items, organic life, and complex organic life, as well as allowing increasingly farther destinations, with Generation 3, Generation 7 and Generation 9 Phase Technology marking major breakthroughs, adding Non-Receiver Based, Multiverse and 4th Time-Space Dimension travel options respectively.

Phase Codes

Phase Codes are sets of alphanumerical strings that allowed Phase Technology to be pointed at a desired destination. G1 and G2 Phase Codes pointed to Phase Receivers located around the Milky Way Galaxy, and G3 or newer Phase Codes pointed to a precise three-dimensional (or in the case of G9 and newer, four-dimensional) coordinate, anywhere within the Universe (G5 and G6), or Multiverse (G7 and newer).

Phase Receivers

Phase Receivers were devices that G1, and G2 Phase Technology could be pointed to, and that items being transported would arrive or depart on.
