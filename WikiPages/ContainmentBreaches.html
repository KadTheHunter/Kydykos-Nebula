Containment Breaches

The Containment Breaches were a series of seven breakouts from the Neurax Barracks by the Neurax Clones contained there.

First Containment Breach

The First Containment Breach occurred one year after the creation of the first Neurax Clone, and resulted in the release of an estimated 730 million Neurax Clones into the Multiverse, and the deaths of more than 800,000 Neurax Barracks Containment Guards.
