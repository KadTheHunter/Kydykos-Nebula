Industrial-Corporate Sector

The Indu-Corp Sector is a section of space occupied exclusively by Factories and Corporations such as Parsa Corp Inc, Lazy Builders Inc., and Neurax Industries.

Location

The Indu-Corp Sector is located inside the F4 grid square of the galactic map, with boundaries extending slightly into F5.

Inhabitants

The Indu-Corp Sector Authority doesn't track individual beings, instead tracking all Companies, Corporations and Factories within its boundaries.

Lazy Builders Inc.
- LBI assembles a customers chosen schematic(s) into a full build, at little or no cost.

Neurax Industries
- Neurax Industries produces and runs a variety of products and services, some at their own expense, others as contracted by / at the expense of, private clients with large orders.

Parsa Corp Inc.
- PCI designs and manufactures a variety of technological components and gadgets, used in everything from common Portable Storage Devices, to the most advanced Phase Technology.

Governance

The Indu-Corp Sector is governed by a body of elected officials known as the Authority. Generally this body consists of the heads of major Companies and Factories, as well as two representatives from each Population Center.

Meetings are held weekly to discuss the state of the Indu-Corp Sector, and address any issues that have arisen since the last meeting; Emergency Meetings can be called at any time when three or more representatives signal for one.
